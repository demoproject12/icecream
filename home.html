Welcome to our
Ice Cream Shop

COLDBERGS was established in the year 1932 , It started out as a dairy business in Mulund, Mumbai. Their obsession with expanding the business resulted in the birth of "Coldbergs's Ice- cream". By the mid-fifties they decided to extend ice cream eating into the non-summer months and in turn, hand-churners were replaced with vertical freezers.

About Us

With Coldberg’s ice-creams available all over India, our dairy products are mainly distributed in Central India.
